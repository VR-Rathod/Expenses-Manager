Welcome to the Daily Expenses Sharing Application

Introduction:

Welcome to our Daily Expenses Sharing Application tutorial. This platform is designed to help you easily manage and share your daily expenses with friends, family, or colleagues. Whether you're splitting bills evenly, calculating exact amounts, or distributing costs based on percentages, our application provides a straightforward solution to keep track of your financial contributions.

With a user-friendly interface, this application simplifies expense management. You'll be able to quickly record and categorize expenses, ensuring that every participant knows their share. Additionally, our platform offers a feature to download detailed balance sheets, making it easy to review and keep records of all transactions.

Get started with this tutorial to learn how to navigate the features of our application and make the most of its capabilities. Our goal is to make expense sharing seamless and transparent, so you can focus on enjoying your time with others without worrying about the details of managing expenses.

Features:

User Management:

Our application requires users to provide essential information such as a unique email address, full name, and a valid mobile number to ensure accurate identification and communication.

Expense Management:

Manage expenses effortlessly with multiple splitting methods: Equal, Exact, and Percentage. Each method allows for flexibility in how expenses are divided, accommodating various group dynamics and preferences.

Expense Calculation Examples:

For an equal split, if you spend a total of 3000 with 3 friends, each person owes 1000. For an exact split, if the total is 4299 and you need to divide it as 799, 2000, and 1500 respectively, this will be accurately calculated. For a percentage split, if you owe 50% of the total and others cover the remaining 50%, the application ensures all percentages sum up to 100%.

Balance Sheet:

The application provides a convenient option to download balance sheets, allowing you to keep a detailed record of all expenses and contributions in a format that suits your needs.
